About Dorohedoro

Dorohedoro is a dark fantasy manga and anime series set in a gritty, lawless city called Hole, where sorcerers experiment on humans. The story follows Caiman, a man with a lizard head and amnesia, as he hunts for the sorcerer responsible for his transformation. The series blends dark humor, intense action, and a unique, chaotic world filled with sorcerers, mutants, and a constant struggle for survival.
