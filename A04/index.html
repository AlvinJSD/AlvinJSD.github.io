About Nintendo Switch

The Nintendo Switch is a hybrid video game console developed by Nintendo. It can be used as a home console connected to a TV or as a portable handheld device. It features a versatile design that allows users to enjoy games anywhere, anytime, with detachable Joy-Con controllers for multiplayer action. With a growing library of games, including iconic titles like The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild and Super Mario Odyssey, the Nintendo Switch offers endless entertainment for gamers of all ages.
