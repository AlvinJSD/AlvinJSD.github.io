Skybound

"Skybound" captures the essence of serenity and vastness, emphasizing the beauty of the skies above. From towering trees reaching toward the blue skies, to the architectural splendor of campus buildings under the expansive afternoon sky, this collection evokes a sense of openness and tranquility. The photographs explore the interplay between nature and human creation, set against the backdrop of an ever-changing sky during the golden hours of the day.
